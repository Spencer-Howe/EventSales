Discover the magic of springtime at Howe Ranch with our Private Farm Tour & Cow Cuddling Experience, specially curated for those seeking an unforgettable encounter with nature and our beloved animals.

✨ Your Private Experience Includes:

  • Facilitated Cow Cuddling Session – Spend quality time with our mini and micro-Highland calves. Enjoy fluffy baby cow hugs, kisses, brushing their beautiful coats, and making connections with these specially trained calves that provide therapeutic relaxation and joyful moments.
