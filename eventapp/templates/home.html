🌼 Private Farm Tour & Cow Cuddling Experience 🌼

An Exclusive Spring Adventure at Howe Ranch – Perfect for Families and Small Groups (1–10 Guests), by appointment starting March 3:

  • Weekdays: 6:00 - 7:30 PM
  • Sundays: 11:30 AM - 1 PM & 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM

$250 for 1–10 Guests

Discover the magic of springtime at Howe Ranch with our Private Farm Tour & Cow Cuddling Experience, specially curated for those seeking an unforgettable encounter with nature and our beloved animals.

✨ Your Private Experience Includes:

  • Facilitated Cow Cuddling Session – Spend quality time with our mini and micro-Highland calves. Enjoy fluffy baby cow hugs, kisses, brushing their beautiful coats, and making connections with these specially trained calves that provide therapeutic relaxation and joyful moments.
  • Photo Opportunities – Capture perfect moments in front of beautifully designed spring-themed backdrops, ideal for sharing and keepsakes.
  • Full Tour of the Ranch – Explore our facilities with guided access to all animal pens. Enjoy hands-on experiences with our alpacas, donkeys, and playful mini goats.

  • Springtime Scavenger Hunt – Embark on a playful adventure across the farm, discovering hidden treasures and winning fun prizes.
  • Highland Cow Sketching & Coloring – Let your creativity flow with Highland cow-themed sketching and coloring supplies, perfect for all ages.

🌸 A Unique, Personal Experience: This exclusive tour is designed for up to 10 guests, ensuring personalized attention and a relaxed pace for you and your group to fully enjoy everything Howe Ranch has to offer. Whether you’re planning an outing around a special occasion or simply seeking an activity filled with joy and connection, this experience is for you.
