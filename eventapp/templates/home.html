🎃 Spooky Moo Time at Howe Ranch
  • 🗓️ Sundays: October 5 and 19
  • 🕒 3:00 PM – 6:00 PM

Celebrate the magic of fall at Howe Ranch during our special Spooky Moo Time open farm days! Bring friends and family for a fun-filled afternoon with our charming micro and mini Highland cows, playful mini goats, alpacas, and friendly donkeys — all set against a festive Halloween backdrop.

✨ Your Spooky Moo Time Includes:

  • 🐮 Cuddles and kisses with our tiny Highland cows — Petunia, Mary Lou, and friends

Celebrate fall your way with a very special private cow cuddling and farm adventure for your date or group!

  • Squeeze our newest tiny fluffy Highland calves, brush and feed the mini herd, and meet alpacas, playful mini goats, donkeys, and new chicks.
